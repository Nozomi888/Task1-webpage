Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

1. Heat oil in a pot and sauté onions until golden brown.
2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
3. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are no longer pink.
4. Stir in the tomato puree, curry powder, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook for 10 minutes.
5. Add water as needed and simmer until the chicken is cooked through.
6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.

Dal Tadka

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup yellow lentils (toor dal)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

1. Cook the lentils with water and turmeric powder until soft.
2. Heat ghee in a pan and add cumin seeds and mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
3. Add onions, green chilies, and ginger-garlic paste. Sauté until onions are golden brown.
4. Add tomatoes, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
5. Pour this mixture over the cooked lentils and mix well.
6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.

Palak Paneer

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer, cut into cubes
  • 4 cups spinach leaves, blanched and pureed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp cream
  • 2 tbsp oil

Instructions:

1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
2. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
4. Add tomato puree, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates.
5. Add the spinach puree and mix well. Cook for 5 minutes.
6. Add the paneer cubes and garam masala. Simmer for 5-7 minutes.
7. Stir in the cream and serve hot with naan or rice.
